About Me
Hi! I'm Mandy and I have 6 years experience tutoring face to face and online. I am a well- experienced Maths, Physics, Design technology and English as a Second Language [ESL] tutor. I have taught to both foundation and higher course level and have recently taught to the new Grade 9-1 cirriculum.
I have a degree in Civil Engineering which requires applied Physics.
I have taught the following
  • GCSE Mathematics - New and old cirriculum - Year 7 to Year 11
  • Further learning Exam preparation - IELTS, UKCAT, TOFEL and GCSE resits
  • Alevel Mathematics: Core 1, Core 2, Core 3, Core 4, Statistics 1,Statistics 2, Mechanics 1 and Mechanics 2 - AQA, Edexcel and OCR
  • AS & A2 Physics
  • Design technology - Product design (Resistant material) Exam prep & Portfolio assistance
  • English Speaking, reading and writing
