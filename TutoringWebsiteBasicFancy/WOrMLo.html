Malcolm Wheeler-Nicholson

Founder of DC Comics 1890 - 1965

According to wikepedia, Major Malcolm Wheeler-Nicholson (January 4, 1890 or January 7, 1890 – 1965) was an American pulp magazine writer and entrepreneur who pioneered the American comic book, publishing the first such periodical consisting solely of original material rather than reprints of newspaper comic strips.

This is a man who lost members in his family since the young age of four. He doesnt seem like the kind of person who asks for trouble. I imagine at the time, going to school and enlisting in the army was the sign of good citizen.
